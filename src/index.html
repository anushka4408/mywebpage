NOVAK DJOKOVIC

Novak Djokovic is a professional tennis player. He is currently world's No.2 in singles by the Association of Tennis Professionals.
Djokovic has been ranked world No.1 for a record total 389 weeks, and finished as the year-end No.1 a record seven times.
